## ‚öñÔ∏è REGULATORY COMPLIANCE FRAMEWORK

### Media Content Regulation (Peru Broadcasting Law)
**Content Aggregation & Republishing Rights**
- **Fair Use Compliance:** Quote extraction and brief summaries fall under journalism exception
- **Source Attribution:** All content must maintain clear attribution to original sources
- **Copyright Considerations:** Transcription and analysis (not full republishing) minimizes risk
- **Commercial Use Licensing:** Negotiate content partnerships with major media outlets where needed

**Implementation Strategy:**
- Focus on factual quote extraction vs full article republishing
- Clear attribution and linking back to original sources
- Transformative analysis and research tools vs content redistribution
- Partnership agreements with willing media outlets for enhanced coverage

### Data Protection & Privacy (Law 29733)
**Media Professional Data Handling**
- **B2B Customer Data:** Standard business contact information, lower sensitivity vs consumer data
- **Usage Analytics:** Anonymized usage patterns for product improvement
- **Content Analysis:** Public domain media content, no personal data extraction
- **Export Compliance:** Customer research exports contain only public domain information

### Competition & Market Practices
**Media Market Concentration Concerns:**
- Platform facilitates journalism (doesn't compete with news creation)
- Enhances existing newsroom capabilities vs replacing journalists
- Supports independent journalists with professional-grade tools
- Transparent pricing without anti-competitive bundling
